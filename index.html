Imola Csóka

Economist | Data Scientist | Contact: imola.csoka@jku.at

I am a lecturer and research assistant at the economics department of the Johannes Kepler University Linz and work as a freelancer Data Scientist. In my research I use applied econometrics to study education and labor economics. Previously, I also participated in data-intensive research projects at the Central European University MicroData research group and at the Corvinus University of Budapest, where I am finishing my PhD studies.

I am passionate about creating value from data and finding patterns that help to understand the society and businesses. I have been involved in teaching and development of various statistics and coding related courses, and motivated to share my enthusiasm for data analysis with others. You can find my CV here.
